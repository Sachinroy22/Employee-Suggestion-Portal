Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Who can submit suggestions?

A: All RINL employees are encouraged to submit ideas for improvement.

Q2: What kind of suggestions are accepted?

A: Suggestions related to cost savings, process improvement, safety, etc.

Q3: Will I get a reward for my suggestion?

A: Yes, impactful suggestions may be rewarded as per RINL policy.

Q4: How is my suggestion evaluated?

A: Suggestions are reviewed by the Suggestion Evaluation Committee.
